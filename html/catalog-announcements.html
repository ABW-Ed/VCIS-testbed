Announcements

The Department of Education (the department) has been notified by a third-party supplier, Instructure, that they have suffered a global cyber security incident, impacting its Canvas Learning Management System.
As a result the department has temporarily restricted access to the Information Sharing and MARAM online Learning System as a precaution.
We will continue to provide further updates here as new information becomes available.
If you have any privacy or security concerns, you can find useful information on the Office of the Victorian Information Commissioner website.
For privacy complaints please contact privacy@instructure.com
