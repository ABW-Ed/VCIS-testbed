Webinar: Child and Family Violence Information Sharing in Practice

This interactive workshop is an open session for education professionals who have completed Information Sharing and/or Child Link training and have further questions they would like to unpack to deepen their understanding. It is an opportunity for participants to bring along questions or challenging situations they have encountered, and to be able to share and discuss these with other eEducation professionals, with guided support from expert facilitators.

Please note this session does not provide training on Information Sharing or Child Link. Please see eLearn and Webinar options for Information Sharing and Child Link training.
