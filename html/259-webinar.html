Webinar: Child and Family Violence Information Sharing in Practice

This interactive workshop is an opportunity for education professionals who have completed Information Sharing and/or Child Link training to deepen their understanding. Participants are encouraged to bring along questions or examples of challenging situations they have encountered to share and discuss with other professionals, guided by expert facilitators.

Please note this session does not provide training on Information Sharing or Child Link. Please see eLearn and Webinar options for Information Sharing and Child Link training.
