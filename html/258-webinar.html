Webinar: Child Link User Training

The Child Link User training course is for nominated Child Link Users. Users will have been nominated by their organisation’s Authoriser.

Child Link is a digital tool that displays consolidated information about a child to authorised professionals who have responsibility for child wellbeing and safety within their organisations.

Child Link User training is facilitated by a team of experienced educators and takes place via interactive webinar.

Training covers a variety of topics, such as:

  • how to log in, use and navigate Child Link
  • responsibilities and obligations when using Child Link
  • how Child Link can add value to professional practice
  • key cultural safety considerations when using Child Link
  • requirements for accessing Child Link safely and appropriately, and
  • where to go for more support and resources.

Participants will complete the Child Link Quiz at the end of the training session, covering the material presented. Successful completion enables activation of a User’s Child Link access.
