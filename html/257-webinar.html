Webinar: Info Sharing and Child Link Training

The Info Sharing + Child Link - Education Training is being offered for people working in schools or education and care services only. If you work in a different workforce, please register for the course called ‘Child Link User Training – All workforces’.
This course is for nominated Child Link Users only. Please only register if you have been nominated by your organisation’s Authoriser.

Training is facilitated by a team of experienced educators and takes place via interactive webinar.

The first part of training, Information Sharing Reforms guides participants through:

  • the intent and purpose of the Information sharing and Family Violence Reforms
  • how the Reforms support and enhance existing child and family wellbeing and safety responsibilities and practices
  • how to apply the Reforms
  • where to go for more support and resources.

The second part of training, Child Link User Training, guides participants through the use of Child Link. It is a digital tool that displays consolidated information about a child to authorised professionals who have responsibility for child wellbeing and safety within their organisations.

This part of training covers topics such as:

  • how to log in, use and navigate Child Link
  • responsibilities and obligations when using Child Link
  • how Child Link can add value to professional practice
  • key cultural safety considerations when using Child Link
  • requirements for accessing Child Link safely and appropriately, and
  • where to go for more support and resources.

Participants will complete the Information Sharing Reforms and Child Link Quiz at the end of the training session, covering the material presented. Successful completion enables activation of a User’s Child Link access.
