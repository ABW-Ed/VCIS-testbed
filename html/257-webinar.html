Webinar: Info Sharing and Child Link Training

The Info Sharing + Child Link - Education Training is being offered for people working in schools or education and care services only. If you work in a different workforce, please register for the course called ‘Child Link User Training – All workforces’.
This course is for nominated Child Link Users only. Please only register if you have been nominated by your organisation’s Authoriser.

Training is facilitated by a team of experienced educators and takes place via interactive webinar.
